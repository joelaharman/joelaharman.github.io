At the consumer electronics show (CES) in 2019, a number of automakers also demonstrated the use of AR while driving.The Genesis G80, for example, tracks the driver's line of sight to ensure that the holographic overlay is always in the right place to ensure the accuracy of AR rendering scenes.Instead of looking down at the GPS panel on the dashboard, the driver sees the arrows on a head-up display that provides real-time direction.Porsche is heavily funding the development of similar technologies.

Head-up displays have been widely used in military aerospace for decades, but thanks to AR technology, head-up displays are only now being used in cars.Displays on the dashboard can be superimposed onto the windshield and directly into the driver's view using AR technology.Drivers can see danger warnings, directions and traffic warnings.In addition, the system can provide information about nearby landmarks and destinations to drivers and passengers.

Swiss-based WayRay is by far the biggest player in the field outside of the big car companies.It used the i-cockpit 3D HUG on a brand new Peugeot 208.Porsche is also in the process of putting the c-series money into HUD construction. Hyundai is already a leader in AR research that goes beyond the driver's cockpit-style perspective.Since 2015, the company has combined collaboration and assistance technology with AR to assist with maintenance under the guidance of experts.Hyundai's app allows users to point their phones at their cars to get information.For example, if you want to know the location of the fuse panel, the application will highlight it on the screen.Mercedes has a similar app, but its version adds a chatbot to provide virtual help. One of the main advantages of ar in cars is that many of the problems that arise in other applications are easily overcome.Cars are already equipped with generators that can generate electricity at any time, regardless of battery capacity.Windshields can also replace bulky headgear.Drivers are also adapting to on-screen data and commands through car-themed video games.
