Apple's AR headset may be released in 2020 in cooperation with Valve

Before the news that Apple has been making AR head project, famous analyst Guo Ming creates also said Apple will launch the next year according to a report by Taiwan media Digitimes, Apple is working with us gaming company Valve to develop an AR headset, which will be launched in 2020. It said Quanta Computer and Pegatron will be responsible for the assembly and supply of apple's AR headset.

For those who don't know much about Valve, Steam is a household name. Yes, Valve created the world's most popular Steam digital store and released the first VR headset Valve Index in April 2019. It is worth mentioning that as early as 2017, apple and Valve cooperated for VR software, and provided native virtual reality (VR) headset support for Valve SteamVR software operating system with macOS High Sierra.

In July this year, it was reported that Apple had suspended the development of AR/VR headset, and the development team was disbanded in May. Apple just changed the development mode of AR/VR headset from independent development to joint development with partners, rather than giving up. The code in iOS 13 was found to show evidence of suspected AR glasses. Even if Apple does not release its brand of AR glasses, it can also announce compatibility with multiple third-party headsets.
